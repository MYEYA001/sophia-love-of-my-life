💌 A Letter Just for You

Happy Birthday Sophia,

Today is your day and even though I am not there in person, I want you to feel how important you are to me through every word of this letter.

Being with you — even from a distance — has meant more than I can properly explain. This kind of connection is not something you just find easily. It is something rare, something real, something that has grown between us with time, trust, patience, and yes, sometimes overthinking, misunderstandings, and distance too. But despite everything, we are still here.

And that means something.

I know things have not always been perfect between us. We have had moments where things felt heavy, moments where we did not fully understand each other, and moments where distance made everything feel harder than it should be. But even then, I never lost sight of what we have.

Because what we have is not just a simple relationship — it is something built on effort, care, and a lot of emotional strength from both sides.

You are someone who matters to me deeply. Not just today, but every day. Your presence in my life, your personality, your thoughts, even the way you overthink sometimes — all of it makes you, you. And that is something I have come to appreciate more than I probably say.

I will not pretend everything is always easy, but I will always be real with you. And being real means telling you that you mean a lot to me, that I care about you, and that despite everything, I still choose us.

On your birthday, I just want you to feel loved, appreciated, and understood. I want you to smile without overthinking. I want you to enjoy your day without stress, even if it is just for a moment.

You deserve that peace.

And maybe one day distance will not be between us anymore. Until then, I am still here — trying, growing, and holding onto what we have built together.

Happy Birthday again, Sophia. I hope this year brings you clarity, happiness, and moments that make you feel truly alive.

Someone who cares about you more than words can fully show,
Amine ❤️
